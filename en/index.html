How the lead process works

The first goal is not to force a booking. It is to give each patient a useful preliminary treatment plan before they travel.

1

Name, country, treatment interest, X-ray if available, photos, and preferred travel month.

2

3

Plan your visit to Tirane

Confirm the consultation, treatment timeline, and next steps before arranging travel.
