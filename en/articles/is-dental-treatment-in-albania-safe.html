Patient safety

Is dental treatment in Albania safe?

Dental treatment abroad can be a good option when patients choose carefully, verify the clinic and ask the right questions before travelling.

What to check first

Ask about dentist qualifications, surgical experience, CE-marked implants, implant passport documentation, sterilization standards, real reviews, before/after cases, and aftercare instructions.

Why credentials matter

Implant treatment is surgical. DentAlba positions complex implant planning around maxillo-facial surgeon expertise, which is especially relevant for full-arch cases.

Do not choose only by price

Low pricing is attractive, but patients should compare the whole plan: diagnosis, materials, timeline, guarantee terms, travel support and what happens after returning home.

Ask before you travel

Send your case and questions on WhatsApp. DentAlba can explain the first planning steps before you book travel.
