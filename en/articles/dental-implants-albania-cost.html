Cost guide

How much do dental implants cost in Albania?

Dental implant cost depends on the number of implants, crowns, bone condition, materials, scans, and whether extra procedures are needed.

Short answer

DentAlba publicly lists All-on-4 at 4860 EUR and All-on-6 at 6060 EUR as package starting points. A personal quote requires X-rays, photos, and clinical review.

What is included in the value?

DentAlba uses CE-marked implants, provides an implant passport for future EU follow-up, offers up to 15 years guarantee according to plan terms, and can support airport pickup, accommodation, and up to 200 EUR flight ticket reimbursement.

What affects the final price?

The biggest factors are bone volume, number of missing teeth, extractions, grafting, implant position, crown material, and whether the case requires a full-arch restoration. Crowns are fabricated in DentAlba's own laboratory using raw materials sourced from Germany.

Why ask for a remote review?

A generic implant price can be misleading. Sending a panoramic X-ray and photos lets the clinic prepare a more useful preliminary treatment plan before you travel.
