Ask for my smile options

Zirconia crowns

Protect and restore broken, weakened or heavily repaired teeth with durable crowns planned for your bite.

Dental veneers

Improve the visible smile line when teeth are discoloured, uneven or worn, where veneers are clinically suitable.

Full smile restoration

Combine crowns, veneers and implant restorations when several teeth need rebuilding for function and aesthetics.

Personalised restorations from DentAlba's own laboratory

German materials

Prosthetists on site

3D mouth scan
