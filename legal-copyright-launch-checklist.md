# DentAlba Copyright And Trust Launch Checklist

## Current Website Status

- The before/after portfolio pages were removed.
- Unused generated case images were deleted from `assets/images`.
- Footer legal notes were added to the public pages.
- Public placeholder messages were removed from homepage and contact footers.
- Remaining visuals are illustrative support materials until DentAlba-approved clinic photos are added.

## Before Launch

1. Replace all illustrative visuals with DentAlba-owned photos when possible.
2. Use before/after patient cases only with written patient consent.
3. Keep copies of photo consent forms, photographer licenses, and asset source records.
4. Confirm the exact WhatsApp number, clinic email, domain name, dentist names, implant brands, guarantee terms, accommodation terms, and reimbursement terms.
5. Add the final domain to canonical tags, hreflang tags, robots.txt, and sitemap.xml once the domain is chosen.
6. Do not copy competitor text, photos, page layouts, reviews, logos, or before/after cases.
7. For review content, use only real patient reviews from sources where reuse is allowed or ask patients for written permission.
8. For medical claims, keep wording conditional: final treatment depends on diagnosis, X-rays, scans, bone condition, and written treatment plan.

## Recommended Photo Policy

- Clinic photos: DentAlba-owned or photographer-licensed only.
- Team photos: written approval from each team member.
- Patient photos: written patient consent, including permission for website and marketing use.
- Before/after cases: no AI cases, no competitor cases, no stock dental transformations.
- Travel/accommodation photos: use DentAlba-owned photos or licensed hotel/partner photos only.

## Highest-Revenue Trust Assets Still Needed

- Three real patient testimonials with permission to publish: name or initials, country, treatment, star rating, and short quote.
- Doctor names, doctor photos, and one credential line for each surgeon or lead clinician.
- Confirm whether `info@dentalba.al` is the correct lead email address. The website email form currently uses this address until the clinic confirms the final inbox.
