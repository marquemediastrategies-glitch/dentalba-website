# 90-Day Organic Execution Plan

## Principle

This plan is built for free lead generation. The target is not traffic alone. The target is qualified inquiries from European patients who are actively researching dental implants, All-on-4, crowns, veneers, or full-mouth restoration in Albania.

No one can honestly guarantee SEO success. The controllable goal is to publish the right pages, build trust, distribute content consistently, measure results, and improve every week.

## Weekly Rhythm

Every week:

- Publish or improve 2 SEO assets.
- Post 3-5 short videos.
- Answer 10 relevant questions in groups/forums/Q&A sites.
- Submit or follow up on 2 directory/backlink opportunities.
- Review Search Console and lead tracker.
- Improve the highest-impression page.

## Month 1: Foundation

Goals:

- Build trust foundation.
- Launch first high-intent pages.
- Start capturing leads.

Tasks:

- Complete `templates/clinic-inputs.md`.
- Create Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Google Business Profile, and Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Publish French and English dental implant landing pages.
- Publish English and French implant cost articles.
- Add WhatsApp tracking links and quote forms.
- Create a basic price guide PDF or page.
- Submit to DentalBnB, AirClinic, Google Business Profile, and Bing Places.
- Start posting short videos in English and French.

Success metrics:

- Website indexed.
- First impressions in Search Console.
- First WhatsApp clicks or form submissions.
- At least 4 trust assets live: photos, dentist bio, reviews, cases.

## Month 2: Multilingual Expansion

Goals:

- Expand into Italian and Spanish.
- Increase topical authority.
- Start earning mentions and backlinks.

Tasks:

- Publish Italian and Spanish dental implant landing pages.
- Publish Albania vs Turkey comparison article.
- Publish All-on-4 English and French treatment pages.
- Publish safety/trust article.
- Answer questions in French, Italian, Spanish, and English communities.
- Pitch Albania travel blogs and expat websites.
- Add patient case study pages.

Success metrics:

- 10-20 indexed pages.
- Leads from at least 2 languages.
- At least 5 directory/community placements.
- Clear top keywords appearing in Search Console.

## Month 3: Optimization

Goals:

- Improve what already gets impressions.
- Convert more visitors into consultations.
- Build authority around Albania dental tourism.

Tasks:

- Add Belgium French and Scandinavian pages.
- Improve pages using Search Console query data.
- Add FAQs based on real lead questions.
- Add internal links between all treatment, cost, comparison, and country pages.
- Create downloadable price guides by language.
- Follow up all backlink and directory outreach.
- Build a monthly report from `tracking/weekly-kpi-dashboard.csv`.

Success metrics:

- Rising impressions and clicks.
- Higher WhatsApp click rate.
- More X-rays/photos received.
- More quotes sent.
- At least one booked consultation/patient from organic channels.

## Conversion Rules

- Every page must have WhatsApp above the fold.
- Every page must offer a free preliminary treatment plan.
- Every treatment page must ask for X-rays/photos.
- Every price page must explain that final pricing depends on diagnosis.
- Every trust page must include real proof, not generic claims.

## Stop Doing

- Generic travel articles with no dental intent.
- Posting only before/after images without a CTA.
- Copying competitors' claims.
- Promising unrealistic prices before diagnosis.
- Publishing pages without a lead form.
