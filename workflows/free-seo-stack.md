# Free SEO And Lead Stack

## Analytics And Search

- Google Search Console: indexing, search queries, clicks, impressions.
- Google Analytics 4: sessions, conversions, country data.
- Bing Webmaster Tools: Bing indexing and extra keyword data.
- PageSpeed Insights: speed and Core Web Vitals checks.
- Google Trends: compare demand by country/language.
- Ahrefs Webmaster Tools: free technical and backlink checks for owned sites.
- Screaming Frog free version: crawl up to 500 URLs.

## Lead Capture

- WhatsApp Business.
- Google Forms, Tally, Typeform free tier, or website-native form.
- Calendly free tier for consultation booking.
- Google Sheets or `tracking/lead-tracker.csv`.

## Content Production

- Google Docs for drafting.
- Canva free tier for simple social visuals.
- CapCut free tier for short video editing.
- YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Reels.

## Distribution

- Google Business Profile posts.
- Facebook groups.
- Quora.
- Reddit where allowed by community rules.
- LinkedIn posts from dentist/clinic owner.
- Medical tourism directories.
- Albania travel blogs and expat sites.

## Measurement

Track weekly:

- Organic sessions.
- Search Console impressions.
- Search Console clicks.
- WhatsApp clicks.
- Form submissions.
- X-rays/photos received.
- Quotes sent.
- Consultations booked.
- Patients booked.
