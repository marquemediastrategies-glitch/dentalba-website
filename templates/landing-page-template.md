## Sections

### Treatment Options

Explain implants, crowns, veneers, All-on-4, All-on-6, or the specific treatment page topic.

### Why Patients Travel To Albania

- More affordable than many Western European countries
- Short flights from many European cities
- Modern dental technology
- Multilingual patient coordination
- Treatment and travel planning support

### Prices

Show ranges, not fake fixed promises.

Example:
The final price depends on the number of implants, bone condition, crown material, scans, and any additional procedures. Send your X-ray or photos for a personalized estimate.

### Treatment Journey

1. Send X-ray/photos.
2. Receive preliminary treatment plan.
3. Video consultation.
4. Travel to Albania.
5. First appointment and scans.
6. Treatment.
7. Follow-up and aftercare instructions.

### Dentist And Clinic Trust

Add dentist bios, credentials, technology, materials, and real photos.

### Before And After

Use real cases with patient consent. Add treatment type, timeline, and patient country where allowed.

### FAQ

- Is dental treatment in Albania safe?

- Can I get a quote before I travel?

- Do you speak my language?
- What happens if I need follow-up after returning home?
