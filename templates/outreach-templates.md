## Quora / Forum Answer Structure

Question:
Is dental treatment in Albania worth it?

Answer outline:

- Yes, for some patients, especially implants, crowns, veneers, and full-mouth restorations.
- The main advantage is cost compared with Western Europe.
- Do not choose only by price.
- Check credentials, reviews, materials, before/after cases, and aftercare.
- Ask for a remote consultation before travel.
- CTA: If you want an estimate, send X-ray/photos for review.
