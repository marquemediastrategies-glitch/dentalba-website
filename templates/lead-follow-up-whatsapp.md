# WhatsApp Lead Follow-Up Workflow

Goal: reply within 5-10 minutes whenever possible.

## Message 1: Immediate Reply

Hello [Name], thank you for contacting [Clinic Name].

We can prepare a free preliminary dental treatment plan for you before you travel to Albania. To make it accurate, please send:

- A panoramic X-ray if you have one
- 2-3 clear photos of your smile/teeth
- The treatment you are considering
- Your country and preferred travel month

Once we review it, we can send you an estimated plan and price range.

## Message 2: If They Ask About Price Only

The price depends on the number of teeth, bone condition, implant/crown type, and whether extra procedures are needed.

We can give you a realistic estimate before you travel if you send an X-ray or clear photos. That way you avoid a misleading generic price.

## Message 3: 24-Hour Follow-Up

Hi [Name], just checking whether you had time to send your X-ray or photos.

Once we receive them, our dentist can prepare a preliminary treatment plan and price estimate for your case.

## Message 4: 3-Day Trust Follow-Up

Hi [Name], if you are still comparing clinics, here are the main things we recommend checking:

- Dentist qualifications
- Real before/after cases
- Implant brands and materials
- Reviews from international patients
- Clear aftercare process
- Written treatment plan before travel

We are happy to review your case and explain the options clearly before you make a decision.

## Message 5: Consultation Invite

Hi [Name], would you like to schedule a short video consultation with our patient coordinator/dentist?

We can explain your options, estimated timeline, and what your trip to Albania would involve.
